About Me

I am a PhD student at Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey, working under the supervision of Prof. Selim Balcisoy. My research sits at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Extended Reality (XR), and Human-AI Interaction, with a focus on how people collaborate with AI systems in immersive environments.


I am particularly interested in how XR technologies can support complex, high-stakes collaborative tasks — such as disaster response — and in designing AI systems that people can appropriately trust and rely on. My work spans both theoretical and applied research, combining user studies, system design, and empirical evaluation.
